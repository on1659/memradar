Promptale Theme Study

Mode first, accent second.

A single accent list for every surface makes the light modes feel washed out and some dark combinations too loud. This mockup treats each mode as its own visual family, then assigns 2-3 accent colors that feel natural on that surface.

Dark surfaces favor cooler accents. Light surfaces need deeper accent shades. Paper works best with warmer or ink-like tones. One accent name can use a different shade per mode.

Combinations to review before shipping as defaults

Light + Mint Too soft on pale backgrounds unless the mint is pushed much darker.
Light + Amber Warm but weak. Buttons and borders start losing hierarchy fast.
Paper + Violet Technically usable, but the temperature clash feels less intentional.
Dark + Amber Great for alerts or highlights, a little loud as the default brand accent.
